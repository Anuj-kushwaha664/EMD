Key Features

  • Employee Records Management: Maintain detailed records of all employees, including personal information, employment history, and performance evaluations.
  • Departmental Management: Tailored functionality for HR, Telecom, and Training departments, ensuring that each department can manage its specific needs effectively.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Secure access to the system with role-based permissions, ensuring that sensitive information is accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor and evaluate employee performance, set goals, and provide feedback to foster continuous improvement.
  • Leave and Attendance Management: Track employee attendance, manage leave requests, and ensure compliance with company policies.
  • Payroll Management: Automate payroll calculations, deductions, and disbursements, reducing errors and ensuring timely payments.
